Her Holiness the Dalai Lama

Here is a list of all Dalai Lamas up to now:

  1. Gedun Drub (1391–1474)
  2. Gedün Gyatso (1475–1542)
  3. Sönam Gyatso (1543–1588)
  4. Yontan Gyatso (1589–1617)
  5. Lobzang Gyatso (1617–1682)
  6. Tsañyang Gyatso (1683–1706)
  7. Kelsang Gyatso (1707–1757)
  8. Jampel Gyatso (1758–1804)
  9. Lungtog Gyatso (1805–1815)
  10. Tshültrim Gyatso (1816–1837)
  11. Kedrub Gyatso (1838–1856)
  12. Trinle Gyatso (1857–1875)
  13. Thubtan Gyatso (1876–1933)
  14. Tenzin Gyatso (born 1935)

Further information regarding the Dalai Lama can be found on Wikipedia.
